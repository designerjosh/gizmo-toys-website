Water Pencils: Expanding Coloring Pencils

$25 00

When we say "expanding," we mean it. Not only do these special coloring pencils last a long time, but they do amazing things when you dip them in water! Just submerge the end of the pencil in clean water for 15 seconds, then apply the pencil to any paper surface without drying, and watch the color expand outward on the page as you draw. These pencils also use half as much color as normal coloring pencils as the water is absorbed into the tip, which means they last twice as long.

Comes with:

64 Water Pencils, 1 small cup.

Dimensions:

Each pencil is 9 inches in length.
