About Gizmo Toys

Our Company

Gizmo Toys Inc. was founded in 2010 by revolutionary inventor Scott Henderson. He wanted to develop a toy company that revolutionized the way children play and develop their creativity. Gizmo first started out as a small toy shop on the corner of Main Street in Westerville, Ohio. It became such a spactacle of the town that Scott decided to take it to newer heights. Since the completion of the Gizmo warehouse in Westerville in 2012, Gizmo Toys has been providing quality toys to children all over the country, with nearly 20 toy shop locations nationwide.

Our Mission

Gizmo Toys Inc. is revolutionizing the way kids use their creativity at play, with the largest collection of hi-tech toys on the market. We take thoroughly tested technology and apply it to the design of our toys in a way that increases children’s creativity while child-proofing every piece for the maximum safety of your child. Innovation and safety are of equal importance to our values here at Gizmo Toys, and it is paramount that every customer, old and new, knows that.

Our Policy

We have a generous 90 day money back guarantee. If your toy breaks or you are not completely satisfied with it, we will grant you a replacement item, a different item of equal or lesser value, or refund your money in full. Gizmo pays return shipping.
